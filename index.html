Key Contracts and Events

Myanmar-Russia Nuclear Power Plant Contract

In 2023, Myanmar and Russia signed a landmark contract to collaborate on building a nuclear power plant. This initiative is set to revolutionize Myanmar's energy sector by introducing advanced nuclear technology and fostering long-term energy security.

Science Festival by Rosatom at Yangon Technological University

Rosatom hosted a science festival at Yangon Technological University in 2023 and 2024. These events showcased advancements in nuclear technology and engaged the academic community in discussions about the future of energy in Myanmar.
